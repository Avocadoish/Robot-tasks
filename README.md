# Robot Tasks

## Algorithm 1: Making Tea

### Input
- Cup
- Tea bag
- Hot water

### Process
1. Pick up the cup.
2. Put the tea bag in the cup.
3. Pour hot water into the cup.
4. Wait for 2 minutes.
5. Remove the tea bag.

### Output
- A cup of tea is ready.

---

## Algorithm 2: Watering a Plant

### Input
- Water
- Plant

### Process
1. Pick up the watering can.
2. Fill the watering can with water.
3. Move to the plant.
4. Pour water into the plant pot.
5. Stop when enough water is given.
6. Put down the watering can.

### Output
- The plant is watered.

---

## Algorithm 3: Turning On a Light

### Input
- Light switch
- Electricity

### Process
1. Move to the switch.
2. Raise robotic arm.
3. Press the switch.
4. Check whether the light is on.

### Output
- The light turns on.
